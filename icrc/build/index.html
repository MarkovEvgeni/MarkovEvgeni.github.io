The results of our meticulous work can be evaluated at the official page of the organization. The resource can be reached from any type of device since it has an adaptive and flawless design.

Our goal in creation of the web-resource for the ICRC was to design and develop a minimalistic but substantial resource, that would reflect the solemnity of the ICRC and its intentions.

The goal of the ICRC's work is to ensure the respect for the life and dignity of victims of armed conflicts and other situations of violence, and ensure their physical and mental integrity through the neutral and independent humanitarian activities.
